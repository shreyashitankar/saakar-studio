What is Saakar?

Be it a wedding, an engagement, a proposal, welcoming a new member to the family, or a kids first birthday party, people always prefer sharing these important personal and candid moments with someone who understands them and who they can share a good comfort level with. Enter Fotowalle, who create a hassle and stress free vibe during shoots to ensure their subjects are at their natural best, which is also the key to their style of creative and candid photography.



“The word Saakar is the actual come from
Sa-Aakar.
Sa : with
Aakar : From ”



This is same spirit and philosophy the extended team of Fotowalle also imbibes. For us, its not just a photo assignment that we take on, infact we are respectful to the fact that people welcome us into their families and homes across the country. Nothing brings us more joy than spreading the love, capturing people at the peak of their emotions and transforming their life’s stories into art…
